## Contact Support

Reach out when the documentation is not enough or something looks wrong with your Grantmaster environment.

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="In-app support" icon="message-square" href="https://support.your-org.com">
    Open the Help panel in Grantmaster and start a chat with the support team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email support" icon="mail" href="mailto:support@your-org.com">
    Send a detailed description of your issue, your role, and recent actions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Status page" icon="alert-triangle" href="https://status.your-org.com" target="_blank">
    Check whether Grantmaster or key services are degraded or under maintenance.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Callout kind="tip">
When you contact support, include your organization name, your Grantmaster role, affected grant or expense IDs, and timestamps. This helps the team resolve issues faster.
</Callout>
