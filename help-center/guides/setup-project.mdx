## Overview

Set up your Grantmaster organization, invite your team with the right roles, and create your first grant project so you can start tracking expenses and journals.

<Callout kind="info">

You must be invited to Grantmaster and signed in with the **ADMIN** or **MANAGER** role to complete this setup.

</Callout>

## Set up your organization and first project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm organization details" icon="settings">
    Review your organization name, legal entity, and primary contact email in the organization settings. Update anything that looks out of date so reports and grant records use the correct details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite your team and assign roles" icon="users">
    Add teammates who will work on grants or finances. Assign roles based on responsibilities: **ADMIN** for org setup and policies, **MANAGER** for approving expenses and journals, **MEMBER** for submitting their own expenses and journals, and **AUDITOR** for read-only access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first grant project" icon="file-plus">
    Create a new grant project with a clear name and funder. Set the grant lifecycle using stages such as discovery, pipeline, submitted, won, active, reporting, and closed so everyone sees where the grant stands.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Define basic tracking and statuses" icon="bar-chart">
    Configure key fields you want to track on the project, such as start and end dates, total award amount, and reporting deadlines. Align project status and stage with your current lifecycle so reports stay consistent.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure approvals for expenses and journals" icon="check-circle">
    Set who approves expenses and journals for this project. Typically, assign a **MANAGER** as the primary approver, with an **ADMIN** as a backup for policy or billing-related reviews.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue with grants and expenses" icon="arrow-right">
    After setup, learn how stages move through the grant lifecycle and how your team submits spending:

    - Read about stages and statuses in the [grants lifecycle FAQ](/help-center/faq/grants-lifecycle).
    - Walk through submitting spending with the [submit an expense guide](/help-center/guides/submit-expense).
  </Step>
</Steps>
